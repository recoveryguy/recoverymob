About
Recovery Mob

An independent recovery resource built by practitioners, for athletes and active people who want to understand the science — not just follow the trend.

Why Recovery Mob Exists

Recovery has become a billion-dollar industry. That's created an enormous amount of content — most of it selling something, most of it light on science, and most of it written by people who understand marketing better than physiology.

Recovery Mob exists because we got tired of that. We're practitioners — people who have spent years in gyms, in ice baths, in saunas, on the floor after hard sessions — and who went deep on the science when we started questioning why some recovery approaches worked and others didn't. What we found was that the research is genuinely good. The problem is the translation from peer-reviewed literature to practical protocol.

This site is that translation. Every guide starts with the mechanism — what is actually happening physiologically — then the evidence — what the research shows — then the protocol — what to actually do. No supplements to sell. No gear commissions. No affiliate links. Just the information.

Who We Are

Recovery Mob is part of the Primal Recovery Centre ecosystem — Melbourne's purpose-built sports recovery facility in Moorabbin. The facility runs ice baths, cryotherapy, infrared sauna, heated magnesium spa, red light therapy, compression boots, vibration platform, and gravity inversion — the full complement of professional-grade recovery modalities under one roof.

Everything written on this site has been tested in practice. The protocols in these guides are the protocols that athletes use at the facility. The science is the science that informed the facility's design and operation. We're not writing about recovery from the outside.

How to Use This Site

Start with the Topics section if you want to understand a specific modality. The guides are standalone — you can go deep on ice baths without reading about compression first, or vice versa. If you want an overview of how multiple modalities work together, the contrast therapy guide covers the stacking logic.

The Blog covers specific questions, current research, and the practical details that don't fit neatly into the core guides. Resources has the curated external links — the research papers, the protocol documents, the tools we actually use and recommend.

If you're in Melbourne and want to experience the full protocol in person, Primal Recovery Centre is where Recovery Mob is based. Day passes and memberships are available. The facility is at Factory 6, 2-6 Independence Street, Moorabbin.
