// What is Recovery Mob

Recovery science,
without the noise.

Most recovery content online is either trying to sell you something or written by someone who's never been through a hard training week. Recovery Mob is different. We're practitioners, athletes, and researchers who've tested this stuff on ourselves and built the guides we wish existed when we started.

The science of recovery is genuinely well-developed. Cold water immersion, heat therapy, photobiomodulation, compression, breathwork — these are not trends. They are documented, peer-reviewed physiological interventions with measurable outcomes. The problem is that most people either don't know the science or don't know how to apply it practically.

That's the gap we're filling. Every guide on this site is built around the published evidence, translated into protocols you can actually use — whether you're running DIY setups at home or accessing a full recovery facility.
