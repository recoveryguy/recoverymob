Protocol Quick Reference

Contrast Protocol
Heat 15–20 min → Cold 2–4 min → Repeat 2–3 cycles. End cold for performance, heat for sleep.

Ice Bath Standalone
2–8°C, 3–10 min post-training. Controlled nasal breathing throughout.

Sauna Standalone
80–90°C, 15–25 min. Minimum 3x/week for cardiovascular adaptation.

Red Light
630–850nm, 10–20 min. Daily use appropriate. Pre or post training.

Compression
20–30 min post cold. Sequential pneumatic, foot to thigh.
